ChordPro Format Guide

Basic Syntax:

  • [Am]word - Chord above word
  • {title: Song Name} - Song title
  • {artist: Artist} - Artist name
  • {key: C} - Song key

Sections:

  • {start_of_verse} - Begin verse
  • {start_of_chorus} - Begin chorus
  • {comment: Note} - Add comment
  • {capo: 3} - Capo position
